What Is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that uses blockchain technology to securely record transactions without relying on a central authority such as a bank. Unlike traditional currencies, cryptocurrencies can be transferred directly between users across the world.

Some of the most popular cryptocurrencies include Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (Tether), BNB, Solana (SOL) and XRP.

How To Keep Your Crypto Safe

  • Use a strong password.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication.
  • Never share your recovery phrase.
  • Beware of fake giveaways.
  • Ignore guaranteed-profit promises.
  • Verify every website before logging in.
  • Keep your apps updated.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links.
Warning

No legitimate cryptocurrency investment guarantees profits. If someone promises fixed daily returns or asks you to recruit others before earning money, it is likely a scam.

Common Cryptocurrency Scams

  • Fake investment websites.
  • Fake customer support.
  • Romance investment scams.
  • Fake trading signals.
  • Ponzi schemes.
  • Phishing websites.
  • Fake wallet applications.
